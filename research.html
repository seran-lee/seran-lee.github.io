About This Project

The Renaissance Codex Project is a collaborative initiative bringing together scholars from digital humanities, medieval studies, and computational linguistics to create a comprehensive digital archive of annotated manuscripts.

By employing cutting-edge image recognition and natural language processing techniques, we aim to decode centuries-old marginalia and reconstruct the intellectual conversations that shaped European thought.
