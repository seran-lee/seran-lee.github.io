I explored psychological blind spots among those marginalized by structural constraints. Based on the Psychology of Working perspective, I examine the career development and the process of attaining Decent Work among underserved populations in Korea. I also conduct research on integrating digital media and AI into counseling to enhance service accessibility and therapeutic effectiveness.
